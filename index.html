Our Story

Muckleford Creek Beef is a family-run regenerative farm located on Muckleford Creek between Castlemaine and Maldon in Central Victoria.

Deane and Marie Belfield raise Hereford/Angus cattle using regenerative agriculture principles — working with nature to build healthy soils, lush pastures, thriving animals, and nutrient-dense food.

Our focus is simple: healthy soils → healthy pastures → healthy cattle → healthy people.

What Makes Us Different

Regenerative Agriculture

We build healthy soil that captures carbon, improves water retention, and supports biodiversity — creating healthier pastures, cattle, and people.

100% Grass-Fed

Our Hereford/Angus cattle eat only pasture their entire lives — never grain, for superior flavour and nutrition.

No Medical Interventions

No growth hormones, routine antibiotics, food dyes, preservatives, Bovaer, or mRNA vaccines — ever.

Certified Organic

Third-party certified under the National Organic Standard — no synthetic chemicals or fertilisers.

Demeter Biodynamic

Goes beyond organic — treats the farm as a living ecosystem using natural preparations for vital, nutrient-dense food.

Beyond Carbon Neutral

First Victorian farm registered under the Emissions Reduction Fund for soil carbon sequestration. We draw down more CO₂ than we emit.

Our Premium Beef

Prepared and cryovaced by a master butcher. Sold in convenient ~20kg mixed boxes containing steaks, slow-cook roasts, mince, and sausages. Customers consistently tell us it's the most flavoursome beef they've ever tasted.
